Welcome, from the Staff at Cummins!

Cummins Behavioral Health Systems, Inc. is a private not-for-profit organization providing behavioral health services in Boone, Hendricks, Marion, Montgomery, Putnam, and surrounding counties in Central and West Central Indiana. Established in 1971, Cummins provides care to persons of all ages in a variety of office and community-based settings. Read More About Cummins >
